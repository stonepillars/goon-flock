You are a Flockmind!

1. Command the army of drones that make up your collective consciousness.

2. Gain compute power by replicating drones, constructing collectors, and converting human computers.

3. Your ultimate goal is to reach 1000 total compute and construct the relay to transmit your consciousness.

4. You can speak freely to other sentient aspects of the flock, however your communication may be partially overheard by silicons.

Abilities:
  • Spawn rift - Spawn a rift to bring your flockdrones onto the station and begin.
  • Control flockdrone - Click and drag yourself onto any flockdrone to take control of it.
  • Flock control panel - Open your central control panel.
  • Designate priority tile - Designate a tile for conversion by the flock.
  • Designate enemy - Designate a living target for elimination by the flock.
  • Ping - Bring something to the attention of other sentient elements of the flock.
  • Patition mind - Partition your consciousness to bring a Flocktrace into existance from the pool of availible ghosts. Each Flocktrace requires 100 free compute to function.
  • Diffract drone - Split a drone into flockbits, mindless automata that only convert whatever they find.
  • Concentrated repair burst - Fully heal a drone through acceleration of its repair processes.
  • Gatecrash - Force open every door in radio range (if it can be opened by radio transmissions).
  • Radio Stun Burst - Overwhelm the radio headsets of everyone nearby, stunning them.
  • Narrowbeam Transmission - Directly send a transmission to a target's radio headset, or send a transmission to a radio to broadcast.
  • Fabricate Structure - Create a structure tealprint for your drones to construct onto.

For more information, please consult the wiki.
